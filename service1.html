Metal Bender Machine Service – PLC and HMI Programming Development

Location Phnom Penh

Our team successfully developed and implemented a complete PLC and HMI program for the metal bender machine, allowing for the selection of various bending shapes. The program includes precise dimension settings controlled by an encoder, enabling the production of up to 20 different shapes. This development ensures flexible, accurate, and efficient operation for a wide range of bending requirements.
